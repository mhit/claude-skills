## 1. Ability Assessment Standards

### Ability Level Interpretation

#### Stage 6-7 (偏差値60以上): Excellent
- Sufficient cognitive capacity for job performance
- High likelihood of handling complex tasks
- Upper 11% of test-takers

#### Stage 4-5 (偏差値45-60): Average to Above Average
- No concerns for typical job roles
- Performance can be enhanced through training and experience
- Represents 54% of test-takers

#### Stage 2-3 (偏差値45以下): Below Average
- Cognitive challenges present
- Careful consideration needed depending on role
- May struggle with complex reasoning tasks

### Combined Stage Interpretation (合計段階)

Total stage = Sum of three ability stages (基礎能力総合 + 言語 + 非言語)

**Uses:**
- Quick overall cognitive ability assessment
- Company tier matching
- Minimum threshold screening

## 2. Personality Dimension Standards (14項目)

### Interpretation Scale

| 偏差値範囲 | 評価 | 解釈 |
|-----------|------|------|
| 60以上 | 非常に強い | Both strengths and risks - evaluate carefully |
| 55-59 | やや強い | Moderately high - good for matching roles |
| 45-54 | 平均的 | No notable characteristics |
| 40-44 | やや弱い | Moderately low - check role requirements |
| 40未満 | 非常に弱い | Very low - potential concern depending on role |

### 14 Dimensions Detailed

#### 対人・協働スタイル (Interpersonal & Collaboration)

**1. 関係構築 (Relationship Building / 対人接触)**
- High (55+): Sociable, good at networking, sales-oriented
- Low (45-): Introverted, prefers small groups or solo work

**2. リーダーシップ (Leadership / 集団統率)**
- High (55+): Takes initiative, decision-maker, management potential
- Low (45-): Follower type, support role oriented

**3. チームワーク (Teamwork / 協調協力)**
- High (55+): Cooperative, adapts well to organizations
- Low (45-): Independent-minded, individual contributor style

**4. サポート (Support)**
- High (55+): Attentive to others, service-oriented
- Low (45-): Self-centered tendency

#### 行動・実行スタイル (Action & Execution)

**5. フットワーク (Mobility)**
- High (55+): Action-oriented, quick decisions, mobile
- Low (45-): Cautious, deliberative

**6. スピード対応 (Speed of Response / スピーディー)**
- High (55+): Fast processor, good at multitasking
- Low (45-): Careful but slower
- ⚠️ WARNING: Too high (60+) risky for pharmacists/admin roles

**7. 変化対応 (Change Adaptation / 予定外対応)**
- High (55+): Flexible, adapts to unexpected situations
- Low (45-): Prefers routines, struggles with change

#### 自律・持続スタイル (Autonomy & Persistence)

**8. 自律的遂行 (Autonomous Execution)**
- High (55+): Self-management, initiative
- Low (45-): Passive, waits for instructions

**9. プレッシャー耐性 (Pressure Tolerance)**
- High (55+): Stress-resistant, handles demanding work
- Low (45-): Weak under pressure
- ⚠️ CRITICAL: Low (45-) is fatal for sales/management

**10. 着実遂行 (Steady Persistence / 着実持続)**
- High (55+): Persistent, methodical
- Low (45-): Easily bored, lacks endurance
- ⚠️ CRITICAL: Essential (55+) for admin/pharmacist roles

#### 創造・分析スタイル (Creativity & Analysis)

**11. 発想・チャレンジ (Innovation & Challenge / 前例ない課題)**
- High (55+): Innovative, challenge-seeking
- Low (45-): Conservative, prefers precedents

**12. 企画構想 (Planning & Ideation / 企画アイデア)**
- High (55+): Creative, good at planning
- Low (45-): Executor type, weak at planning

**13. 問題分析 (Problem Analysis)**
- High (55+): Logical thinking, analytical
- Low (45-): Intuitive, struggles with analysis

**14. [Varies by Report]**

## 3. Organizational Fit Standards (4つの風土タイプ)

### Stage Interpretation

- **Stage 5**: Very high fit (ideal match)
- **Stage 4**: High fit (good match)
- **Stage 3**: Neutral (no issues)
- **Stage 2**: Requires effort (potential mismatch)
- **Stage 1**: Poor fit (strong mismatch)

### Four Culture Types

#### 創造重視風土 (Creative Culture)
**Characteristics**: Encourages challenges, open communication, innovative, transparent
**Suitable companies**: IT, advertising, trading companies, startups
**Suitable personalities**: Sociable, creative, flexible, novelty-seeking
**Required traits**: 関係構築↑, 発想・チャレンジ↑, 変化対応↑

#### 結果重視風土 (Results Culture)
**Characteristics**: High performance demands, competitive, clear goals, demanding
**Suitable companies**: Foreign firms, finance, consulting, sales organizations
**Suitable personalities**: Rational, autonomous, high achievement drive, competitive
**Required traits**: 達成意欲↑, プレッシャー耐性↑, 自律的遂行↑

#### 調和重視風土 (Harmony Culture)
**Characteristics**: Values people, warm, family-like, supportive
**Suitable companies**: Japanese corporations, service industry, public sector
**Suitable personalities**: Cooperative, relationship-focused, stability-seeking
**Required traits**: チームワーク↑, サポート↑, 従順性↑

#### 秩序重視風土 (Order Culture)
**Characteristics**: Values discipline, careful, functional, planned, clear rules
**Suitable companies**: Banks, manufacturing, government, traditional firms
**Suitable personalities**: Meticulous, steady, rule-following, accuracy-focused
**Required traits**: 着実遂行↑, 慎重性↑, 問題分析↑

## 4. Warning Mark Interpretation

### Severity Levels

#### *** (3 Stars): Critical Concern
- May be deal-breaker depending on role
- **MUST** deep-dive in interview
- Recommend against hiring if cannot address
- Example: ***慎重さの不足 × Pharmacist = Fatal (medical error risk)

#### ** (2 Stars): Significant Concern
- Risk factor for job performance
- Verify in interview, assess addressability
- Example: **目標の低さ × Sales = Serious (won't meet targets)

#### * (1 Star): Minor Concern
- Worth confirming
- Touch on in interview if time permits
- Example: *発想の不足 × Admin = Acceptable (routine work focus)

### Warning Mark Handling Protocol

1. **Identify immediately** at analysis start (never defer)
2. **Always include** in "懸念される特性" section
3. **Set Priority S** for interview questions
4. **Assess risk** in combination with role

### Common Warning Marks by Role Impact

| Warning | Pharmacist | Sales | Admin | Engineering |
|---------|-----------|-------|-------|-------------|
| 慎重さの不足 | ❌ Fatal | ⚠️ Concern | ❌ Fatal | ⚠️ Moderate |
| 目標の低さ | ⚠️ Concern | ❌ Fatal | ✅ OK | ⚠️ Concern |
| プレッシャー耐性低 | ❌ Fatal | ❌ Fatal | ⚠️ Concern | ⚠️ Moderate |
| 発想の不足 | ✅ OK | ⚠️ Concern | ✅ OK | ⚠️ Concern |

## 5. Teachability Assessment (素直さ評価)

### Five Assessment Indicators

| 指標 | 素直さ高い | 素直さ低い |
|------|----------|----------|
| 従順性 | 50以上 | 50未満 |
| 懐疑思考性 | 50以下 | 50以上 |
| 批判性 | 50以下 | 50以上 |
| 独自性 | 50以下 | 50以上 |
| 自己尊重性 | 50以下 | 50以上 |

### Rating Scale

- **◎ (Very High)**: 4-5 indicators match
  - Easy to coach and train
  - Adapts well to organization
  
- **○ (High)**: 3 indicators match
  - Generally receptive
  - Standard coaching works

- **△ (Moderate)**: 1-2 indicators match
  - Somewhat strong-willed
  - Requires tailored coaching approach

- **× (Low)**: 0 indicators match or opposite
  - Stubborn, resistant to coaching
  - May struggle with organizational adaptation

### Report Text Indicators

**High Teachability:**
- "自分の意見や考えにあまりこだわらず" (not attached to own opinions)
- "人の意見を尊重する" (respects others' opinions)

**Low Teachability:**
- "自分の視点を大切にする" (values own perspective)
- "批判的" (critical)

**Important Note**: Mid-career hiring (SPI3-G) standards tend to be more lenient than new graduate standards, as experience compensates for cognitive scores.

### Deviation Score Statistics

The deviation score system follows a normal distribution:
- Mean: 50
- Standard deviation: 10
- Formula: Deviation Score = 50 + 10 × (Individual Score - Mean) / SD

**Key thresholds:**
- 60: Top 34% (one SD above mean)
- 70: Top 2-3% (two SD above mean)
- 40: Bottom 34% (one SD below mean)

This enables fair comparison across different test versions and populations.
