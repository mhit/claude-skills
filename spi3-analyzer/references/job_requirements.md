## 1. SPI3-G Assessment Framework

### SPI3-G's Position in Hiring Decisions
- **Reference material only** (20-30% weight in total evaluation)
- Must be combined with: interviews, work history, references, practical tests
- **NEVER make hire/no-hire decisions based solely on SPI3**

### Response Reliability Indicators

**「自分をよく見せようとする傾向がある」(Tendency to present favorably)**
- Detected by lie scales (矛盾検出) - contradictory responses
- Indicates low response reliability

**Severity Levels:**

**軽度 (Mild: 1-2 contradictions)**
- Normal range of variation
- No significant concern

**中度 (Moderate: Multiple contradictions)**
- Verify personality in interview thoroughly
- May indicate weak self-awareness

**重度 (Severe: Widespread contradictions)**
- Possible intentional impression management
- Approach hiring cautiously - elicit truth in interview

## 2. Job Type Weighting Guidelines

### Ability vs. Personality Weight by Role Type

| 職種タイプ | 能力 | 性格 | 理由 |
|----------|------|------|------|
| 技術職・専門職 (Technical/Specialist) | 60% | 40% | Professional competence is paramount |
| 一般職・事務職 (Administrative) | 40% | 60% | Fit and sustainability matter most |
| 営業・接客職 (Sales/Customer-facing) | 30% | 70% | Personal evaluation is most critical |
| 管理職 (Management) | 50% | 50% | Both require high levels |

**Application Guidelines:**
- Use these weights when making final trade-off decisions
- A sales candidate with Stage 4 ability but perfect personality fit > Stage 6 ability with poor fit
- A technical candidate with Stage 6 ability but moderate personality fit > Stage 4 ability with perfect fit
- Management candidates need both - neither can be significantly weak

## 3. Pharmacist (薬剤師) ⚠️ Highest Caution Required

### Overview
Pharmacists require the most careful evaluation due to patient safety implications. Errors can result in medical accidents, injury, or death.

### Critical Traits (Must-Have: 55+ Recommended)

**着実遂行 (Steady Persistence / 着実持続)**
- Why critical: Must complete tasks accurately without errors
- Low score risk: Mistakes in dispensing, dosage calculation
- Minimum: 55 (strongly recommended)

**慎重性 (Cautiousness)**
- Why critical: Hasty decisions lead to medical errors
- Low score risk: Overlooking contraindications, drug interactions
- Minimum: 55 (strongly recommended)
- ⚠️ Below 40 is **absolute deal-breaker**

**プレッシャー耐性 (Pressure Tolerance)**
- Why critical: Medical settings involve constant stress and tension
- Low score risk: Errors increase under pressure
- Minimum: 50 (recommended 55+)

**問題分析 (Problem Analysis)**
- Why critical: Must evaluate complex prescriptions logically
- Low score risk: Misses critical clinical considerations
- Minimum: 50

### Important Traits (Should-Have: 50+ Recommended)

- **自律的遂行 (Autonomous execution)**: Self-management in fast-paced environments
- **チームワーク (Teamwork)**: Collaboration with doctors, nurses
- **サポート (Support)**: Patient counseling and explanation

### Risk Factors (Disqualifying Conditions)

**⚠️ Absolute Deal-Breakers:**
- **慎重さの不足 (40未満)**: FATAL - medical error risk - **原則NG（不採用推奨）**
- **スピード対応が高すぎる (60以上)**: RISKY - rushing leads to errors
- **フットワークが高すぎる (60以上)**: RISKY - impulsive actions
- **回避性が高い (60以上)**: CONCERN - avoiding responsibility

**Concerning Combinations:**
- Low 慎重性 + High スピード対応 = Dangerous pattern - rushes without thinking
- Low 着実遂行 + Low プレッシャー耐性 = Cannot sustain quality under stress
- High 回避性 + Any other risk = Escapes from responsibility when errors occur

### Ability Requirements

- **基礎能力総合**: Stage 4+ (偏差値45+)
- **非言語能力**: Important for dosage calculations and logical reasoning
- Lower stages acceptable if compensated by exceptionally strong personality fit

### Seeckt Classification Fit
- **Best**: 参謀 (Capable × Diligent) - Meticulous, detail-oriented, thorough
- **Acceptable**: 連絡将校 (if very high in critical traits, especially 慎重性 and 着実遂行)
- **Avoid**: 指揮官 (too fast, efficiency-focused), 要注意 (incapable × diligent)

## 4. Sales Roles (営業職)

### 4A. New Business Sales (新規開拓営業)

#### Critical Traits (Must-Have: 55+)

**関係構築 (Relationship Building / 対人接触)**
- Why critical: Job is fundamentally about connecting with people
- Low score risk: Cannot build rapport with prospects
- Minimum: 55

**プレッシャー耐性 (Pressure Tolerance)**
- Why critical: Targets, rejection, quotas create constant pressure
- Low score risk: Burns out, avoids challenges
- Minimum: 55
- ⚠️ Below 45 is deal-breaker

**達成意欲 (Achievement Drive)**
- Why critical: Must be driven to hit targets and close deals
- Low score risk: Lacks motivation to pursue aggressively
- Minimum: 55
- ⚠️ Below 45 is deal-breaker

#### Important Traits (Should-Have: 50+)
- **フットワーク (Mobility)**: Volume of activity drives results
- **変化対応 (Change adaptation)**: Handling unexpected situations
- **自律的遂行 (Autonomous execution)**: Self-management, minimal supervision

#### Risk Factors
- **達成意欲が低い (45未満)**: Not suited for sales - **営業向いていない（NG）**
- **関係構築が低い (45未満)**: Difficult customer acquisition - **要検討**
- **回避性が高い (60以上)**: Runs from challenges and difficult conversations
- **敏感性が高すぎる (60以上)**: Rejection hurts too much, cannot recover

#### Ability Requirements
- **基礎能力総合**: Stage 4+ (Stage 5+ for complex B2B sales)
- Communication and persuasion require cognitive capacity

#### Seeckt Classification Fit
- **Best**: 指揮官 (Capable × Efficient) - Quick decisions, big picture, results-focused
- **Acceptable**: 参謀 (if also high in action traits like フットワーク)
- **Avoid**: 要注意 (lacks competence), Low 達成意欲 (lacks drive)

### 4B. Account Management Sales (既存顧客・ルート営業)

#### Critical Traits (Must-Have: 55+)

**サポート (Support)**
- Why critical: Sensing and responding to customer needs
- Relationship maintenance over new acquisition

**チームワーク (Teamwork / 協調協力)**
- Why critical: Long-term relationship sustainability
- Collaborative approach to customer success

**着実遂行 (Steady Persistence)**
- Why critical: Consistent follow-up and service delivery
- Building trust over time

#### Important Traits (Should-Have: 50+)
- 関係構築 (Relationship building)
- 変化対応 (Change adaptation)
- プレッシャー耐性 (less critical than new business, but still important)

#### Risk Factors
- Too high フットワーク can harm existing relationships (appears unstable)
- Need balance of action and patience
- Less tolerance for high 回避性 than new business (but slightly more acceptable)

#### Ability Requirements
- Stage 4+ sufficient for most account management roles

#### Difference from New Business Sales
- More relationship-focused, less aggressive
- Service orientation > hunting orientation
- 新規開拓営業ほどの高プレッシャー耐性は不要
- むしろ高すぎるフットワークは既存顧客に悪影響

## General Guidelines Across All Roles

### Universal Risk Factors
- **High 回避性 (60+)**: Concern for all roles - tendency to avoid challenges
- **Extreme scores (70+ or 30-)**: Often indicate risks regardless of trait
- **Multiple warning marks**: Cumulative concern requiring deep investigation

### Ability vs. Personality Priority

**Ability-heavy roles**: Engineering, Analyst, Strategic roles
- Cognitive capacity is primary filter
- Personality provides refinement

**Personality-heavy roles**: Sales, Customer Support, Service
- Personality traits are primary filter
- Ability provides baseline competence

**Balance required**: Management, Pharmacist, Project Management
- Both ability and personality equally critical
- Mismatch in either dimension can be fatal

### Cultural Considerations

Japanese workplace norms prioritize:
- **チームワーク** (Teamwork): Almost universal requirement
- **サポート** (Support): Valued across roles
- **従順性** (Obedience): Easier adaptation to hierarchy
- **着実遂行** (Persistence): "Gambaru" cultural value

Be cautious about:
- Very low チームワーク (organizational friction)
- Very low 従順性 (difficulty with hierarchy)
- Very high 独自性 + low 従順性 (resistance to conformity)

## Critical Reminders

1. **Never use SPI3 alone** - it's 20-30% of the decision
2. **Warning marks are not automatic disqualifiers** - assess in context of role
3. **Cultural fit matters** - Japanese workplace values differ from Western norms
4. **Development potential** - some traits can be coached, others cannot
5. **Role-specific requirements** - same trait can be strength in one role, weakness in another

## General Guidelines Across All Roles

### Universal Risk Factors
- **High 回避性 (60+)**: Concern for all roles - tendency to avoid challenges
- **Extreme scores (70+ or 30-)**: Often indicate risks regardless of trait
- **Multiple warning marks**: Cumulative concern requiring deep investigation

### Ability vs. Personality Priority

**Ability-heavy roles**: Engineering, Analyst, Strategic roles
- Cognitive capacity is primary filter
- Personality provides refinement

**Personality-heavy roles**: Sales, Customer Support, Service
- Personality traits are primary filter
- Ability provides baseline competence

**Balance required**: Management, Pharmacist, Project Management
- Both ability and personality equally critical
- Mismatch in either dimension can be fatal

### Cultural Considerations

Japanese workplace norms prioritize:
- **チームワーク** (Teamwork): Almost universal requirement
- **サポート** (Support): Valued across roles
- **従順性** (Obedience): Easier adaptation to hierarchy
- **着実遂行** (Persistence): "Gambaru" cultural value

Be cautious about:
- Very low チームワーク (organizational friction)
- Very low 従順性 (difficulty with hierarchy)
- Very high 独自性 + low 従順性 (resistance to conformity)
